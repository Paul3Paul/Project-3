Mahatma Gandhi

1869-1948

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi Jayanti is celebrated every year on 2nd October in honor of the birthday of the Father of the nation, Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, popularly known as Mahatma Gandhi who was born on this day in 1869. Fondly remembered as Bapu, Mahatma Gandhi led the principle of non violence and also inspired movements for civil rights, freedom and non violence across the globe.
